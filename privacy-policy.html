Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

StudyReward ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other information you voluntarily provide through our contact form or newsletter subscription.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent, and browser type.
  • Device Information: IP address, operating system, and browser type for analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about new clinical trials and updates
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@studyreward.com.
